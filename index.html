This is not financial advice. It is an educational tool. Signals, buy/sell zones and targets are mechanical calculations on historical data (they can be wrong). The 1–10 confidence is a hypothetical conviction score — how aligned the indicators are — not a probability of making money; even a 10/10 can fail. Whale activity is estimated from volume & On-Balance Volume, not real wallet tracking. The market regime comes from the 200-day average. The weekly trend is factored in only lightly. The backtest is a simplified historical simulation (long-only, no fees/slippage) shown so you can see how the signals behaved — it is not a promise of future results. The decision is always yours; never risk what you can't afford to lose.
